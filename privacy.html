Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Short version: 21 Pro doesn't collect personal data, doesn't sell your information to anyone, and doesn't require an account. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

21 Pro ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it's used, and your rights regarding that information when you use the 21 Pro iOS application.

Data We Collect

21 Pro is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information.

Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Game progress, session history, and statistics
  • App preferences and settings
  • Purchase entitlements (managed by Apple)

This data lives exclusively on your iPhone or iPad and is never sent to our servers.

Purchases & Payments

All in-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not handle, store, or have access to any payment or billing information. Apple's privacy policy governs all transaction data.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as feature interaction counts and session frequency) through Apple's App Store analytics framework. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and is used solely to improve the app experience.

Advertising

The free tier of 21 Pro may display ads served through Apple's advertising platform. We do not build user profiles for advertising purposes, and Edge subscribers see no ads whatsoever. We do not sell, share, or rent any user data to advertisers.

Children's Privacy

21 Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can remove it.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the app from your iPhone or iPad. Deleting the app removes all locally stored game data.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your data including access, correction, and deletion. Since we do not collect personal data, there is typically nothing for us to provide or delete — but if you have concerns, please reach out.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out at support@21proapp.com. We're a one-person operation — real responses from a real human.
